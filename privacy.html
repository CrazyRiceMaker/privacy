Privacy Policy

Evolve Privacy Policy

Evolve is a fitness planning and workout tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains how Evolve collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information and health-related data. By using Evolve, you acknowledge and agree to this Privacy Policy.

App Name: Evolve Developer: Evolve Developer Effective Date: March 12, 2026

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, the app may receive your Apple user identifier, name, and email address.
  • Health Data: With your permission, the app may read your height, weight, body fat percentage, biological sex, date of birth, and heart rate samples from Apple Health.
  • Workout and Plan Data: This includes your training goals, workout frequency, workout records, plan schedules, onboarding answers, and any body data you manually enter or edit in the app.
  • Calendar Data: With your permission, the app may create workout reminder events in your system calendar.
  • Local Device Data: The app stores certain information locally on your device, such as sign-in status, theme preference, guest mode status, and user preferences.

How We Use Information

  • To generate and display personalized workout plans, training recommendations, and progress views.
  • To sync your workout records and profile data across your Apple devices when you use the same iCloud account.
  • To write completed workout records to Apple Health so you can view them within Apple's health and fitness ecosystem.
  • To create calendar reminders when you grant calendar permission.
  • To maintain basic app functionality, such as remembering your session state, guest mode choice, and interface settings.

Permissions and Health Data

  • Health data is only read from or written to Apple Health after you explicitly grant permission.
  • HealthKit data is used solely to provide health, fitness, workout tracking, and related in-app features. It is not used for advertising, marketing, or cross-app tracking.
  • If you do not grant Apple Health access, some personalized features may be limited, but you may still manually enter certain body data in the app.
  • Calendar permission is used only to create workout reminder events. Evolve does not read your full calendar contents.

Data Storage and Sync

  • Some data is stored locally on your device, such as sign-in state, guest mode markers, theme settings, and local preferences.
  • Workout-related data may be synced through Apple's iCloud / CloudKit private database so that your data can be restored across your own Apple devices.
  • If you use the app in guest mode, some data may remain only on the current device and may not be recoverable after uninstalling the app, clearing device data, or switching devices.

Sharing and Third Parties

  • Except for Apple services required to provide Sign in with Apple, Apple Health integration, CloudKit sync, and calendar functionality, we do not sell your personal information to unrelated third parties.
  • Based on the current version of the app, Evolve does not integrate third-party advertising SDKs and does not perform cross-app tracking based on personal data.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or if necessary to protect the legal rights, safety, or property of users, the developer, or the public.

Your Choices

  • You may choose not to use Sign in with Apple and instead use the app in guest mode if that option is available.
  • You may disable Apple Health or calendar permissions at any time in your device settings.
  • You may edit some body data inside the app and may stop using the service by signing out, deleting the app, or managing related iCloud data on your device.
  • If you have privacy questions or requests, you may contact the developer using the contact information below.

Children's Privacy

If you are a minor, please use Evolve only after your parent or legal guardian has reviewed and agreed to this Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time if app features, data practices, or legal requirements change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
